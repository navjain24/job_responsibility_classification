
 
  Embedded in a worldwide network Mercedes-Benz Research & Development North America continuously strives to remain at the forefront of successful automotive research and development. MBRDNA is headquartered in Silicon Valley, California, with key areas of Autonomous Driving, Advanced Interaction Design, Digital User Experience, Machine Learning, Customer Research, and Open Innovation. In Farmington Hills, Michigan, the focus is on Powertrain and eDrive technology as well as in Long Beach, where the teams test durability of the latest driver assistant and telematic systems. The Digital Hub in Seattle focusses on developing a cloud architecture and building out the cloud platform for the next generation of connected car services. The Testing and Regulatory Affairs Division in Ann Arbor and the Advanced Vehicle Design in Carlsbad complete the competence center.
 
 
 
   Mercedes-Benz Seattle Hub is looking for an experienced Infrastructure Development Engineer to grow their career at our office in Seattle. The Infrastructure Development & Cloud Automation team focuses on building, maintaining, and automating the infrastructure for web services and applications that support our cloud business. We are engineers, developer, architects and SMEs with opportunities to develop software to support our charter. We are a geo-diverse team, developing an infrastructure platform to ensure operational stability for our software deployments, the development environment and delivery partners in their adoption of cloud based solutions and datacenters for Mercedes-Benz.
  
 Job Responsibilities:
 
   Independently take product design specifications and deliver a well-defined design, implementation, and testable solution
   Demonstrate knowledge of multiple cloud environments and deployment methodologies
   Cultivate relationships with developers, engineers, and other technology partners to understand their infrastructure needs and deliver solutions
   Provide mentorship and technical direction to associate team-members
   Evangelize best practices and methodologies 
  Identify opportunities to automate processes and procedures
   Provide scalable engineering and supportability deliverables, resulting in products and services with more efficiency, lower risk, and reduced customer impact
   Identify potential security and performance issues at multiple layers of deployment, from hardware, operating environment, network and application
   Support deployment of Kubernetes infrastructure and containers hosted within cloud platforms
   Provide technical expertise in identifying issues that affect availability and quality across the software product portfolio
 
  Minimum Qualifications;
 
   Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science OR Bachelor’s degree in a related field
   2-4 years of professional experience with DevOps practices
   Fluency in one or more programming or scripting languages, with the ability to review and understand workflow and language
   Some level of proficiency in error analysis
   Understanding of the infrastructure lifecycle, from requirements to design, to implementation, testing, environment set-up, multi-phase builds, and deployment approaches
   Understanding of Configuration Management approaches, including tradeoffs involved with different solutions
   Effective experience with Cloud-based Architecture, Services, and Provisioning
   High level proficiency in the CI/CD process, including effective understanding of pipelines, provisioning, testing, and troubleshooting
   Fundamental understanding of IaC (Infrastructure as Code), along with effective experience with at least one tool in this area
   Understanding of instrumentation, logging levels, and system/software troubleshooting
   Advanced understanding of Operating System components.
   Strong written and oral communications skills with the ability to clearly articulate complex concepts
   Excellent troubleshooting skills, with analytical, creative, and innovate approaches to solving problems with the drive to see an issue through to conclusion
   Valid US Driver’s License
 
  Preferred Qualifications:

   Scripting skills (PowerShell, Python, Bash)
