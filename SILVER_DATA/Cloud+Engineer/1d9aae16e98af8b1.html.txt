  GENERAL SUMMARY:
  
  
  As an Infrastructure Engineer at our MSP, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining the core IT infrastructure for our clients. You will work collaboratively with our team of experts to ensure the seamless operation, security, and reliability of our customers' IT environments. Your expertise in VmWare, Networking, Windows Server, VPNs, Switch Configuration, and Firewalls will be essential in creating and managing robust infrastructures that meet the unique needs of each client.
  
  
  Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
 
  
   Design and implement virtualized environments using VmWare to optimize performance and resource utilization for client systems. 
  Configure and manage network components, including routers, switches, firewalls, and VPNs, to ensure secure and efficient communication between systems and networks. 
  Administer Windows Server environments, handling installations, updates, patches, and troubleshooting to maintain high availability and system performance. 
  Collaborate with project managers and clients to understand infrastructure requirements and design appropriate solutions to meet their business needs. 
  Conduct regular system audits and performance monitoring to proactively identify potential issues and optimize system performance. 
  Implement security best practices and ensure compliance with industry standards to safeguard client data and infrastructure. 
  Provide tier 4 support for escalated issues related to infrastructure, collaborating with other teams to resolve complex technical challenges. 
  Document infrastructure configurations, changes, and procedures to maintain accurate and up-to-date records. 
  Stay updated with the latest technologies and industry trends to recommend innovative solutions and process improvements.
 
  
  
  Knowledge, Skills, and/or Abilities Required:
 
  
   Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent work experience). 
  Proven experience as an Infrastructure Engineer or similar role, preferably in a Managed Service Provider environment. 
  Strong expertise in VmWare virtualization technologies and implementation. 
  In-depth knowledge of networking concepts, including TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VLANs, routing, and switching. 
  Proficiency in configuring and managing Windows Server environments (2012, 2016, 2019). 
  Experience with VPN technologies and remote access solutions. 
  Familiarity with firewall administration and security best practices. 
  Excellent problem-solving skills with the ability to diagnose and resolve complex technical issues. 
  Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively in a remote team environment.
