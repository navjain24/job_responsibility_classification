Key job responsibilities:

  • Provision and manage AWS infrastructure, including EC2, CloudFormation Templates, AppStream, and Load Balancers.
  • Build dash boarding and alarming to drive Operational Excellence.
  • Work with a wide range of technologies, and AWS products.
  • Build AWS Application Environments including VPC, EC2, RDS, SSM, S3.
  • Architect and setup infrastructure on AWS and AWS gov-cloud environments.
  • Analyze and Support setup of new devices connectivity to applications.
  • Monitor network connectivity between all internal and external systems.
  • Work closely with Application leads to understand needs and plan for infrastructure demands.
  • Drive automation of routine tasks to help scale the operations.
  • Maintain hardware architecture documentation for all infrastructure and networking components.
  • Support Identity and Access Management (IAM) initiatives building highly secure working environments.
  • You will work collaboratively with internal security teams and key stakeholders to intake requirements and deliver a customer obsessed IAM experience.

Basic Qualification

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Systems Engineering, Computer Science or related field, or relevant work experience.
  • 4+ years of experience in Systems and Network Administration, DevOps, or Site Reliability Engineering.
  • 4+ years of experience with focus in areas such as systems, network and/or application security.
  • 4+ years of experience with web protocols and Linux/Unix tools and architecture.
  • 4+ year experience building scripts, tooling, and automation for large-scale computing environments using Python, Java, PHP, Ruby, or Bash/Shell.
  • Experience communicating with technical and non-technical stakeholders at all levels across multiple business units.
  • Demonstrable track record dealing well with ambiguity, prioritizing needs, and delivering measurable results in an agile environment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of experience in Systems and Network Administration, DevOps, or Site Reliability Engineering.
  • Demonstrable expertise around specifying, designing, and/or implementing system health, performance monitoring tools, and software management tools for 24x7 environments.
  • Practical knowledge of Role-based identity management including SPML and SAML protocols for provisioning.
  • Experience in the Identity and Access Management field with Practical experience in LDAP and AD implementations in support of enterprise directory services.
  • Familiarity with AWS Cloud Security controls and deployment
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and development of creative solutions
  • Understanding of design for scalability, performance, and reliability.
  • Automation, testing or monitoring framework development experience.
  • Understanding of common network and federated authentication protocols like Kerberos, OAUTH, NTLM, OIDC, SAML, FIDO, etc.
  • Expertise in directory schema design and data integration
