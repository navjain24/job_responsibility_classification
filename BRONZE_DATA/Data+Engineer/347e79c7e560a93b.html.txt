   The Role
  
  
  
    We are seeking a passionate and self-driven Senior Data Engineer to join our data science team. As a Senior Data Engineer, you will play a crucial role in building and maintaining robust data ingestion pipelines, performing feature engineering, and facilitating knowledge transfer within the team. Your expertise in data acquisition, ETL pipelines, and modern data lake architectures will be essential in supporting our data-driven initiatives and enhancing our data science capabilities.
  
  
 
  
   What Youâ€™ll Do
   
    
      Build and maintain data ingestion pipelines: Design, develop, and optimize scalable and efficient data ingestion pipelines. Ensure the pipelines are reliable, secure, and capable of handling large volumes of data from various sources.
      Perform feature engineering: Collaborate with the data science team to identify, extract, and transform relevant features from raw data sources. Implement feature engineering techniques to enhance the quality and usefulness of the data for modeling and analysis purposes. Make those features available to online and offline processes.
      Provide knowledge transfer and support: Share your expertise in writing pipelines and leveraging our data lake with the wider data science team. Conduct training sessions, workshops, or one-on-one mentoring to help team members enhance their skills and understanding of pipeline development and data lake usage.
      Collaborate with cross-functional teams: Work closely with data scientists, data analysts, and other stakeholders to understand their requirements and provide technical solutions. Participate in discussions and contribute ideas for optimizing data engineering processes and enhancing data-driven decision-making.
      Ensure data quality and security of your work: Work closely with our Data management team to implement best practices and standards for data quality assurance, data governance, and data security. Identify and address any data-related issues or anomalies that may impact the accuracy and reliability of analytical outcomes.
      Stay updated with industry trends: Keep up-to-date with the latest advancements in data engineering, data science, and related technologies. Share knowledge and insights with the team to drive innovation and continuous improvement.
    
   
  
  
 
  
   Who You Are
   
    
      Excellent communication and collaboration skills to work effectively in a cross-functional team environment.
      Strong problem-solving skills and ability to troubleshoot data-related issues.

      Excellent programming skills in Python, SQL, or other relevant languages.
