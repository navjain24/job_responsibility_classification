 Project Kuiper is an initiative to launch a constellation of Low Earth Orbit satellites that will provide low-latency, high-speed broadband connectivity to unserved and underserved communities around the world. 

 The Role 
We are looking for a skilled and enthusiastic engineer to support our team of Payload integration and verification engineers as we build next-generation communications systems for space. In this fast-paced role, you will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the related infrastructure used by these teams to create a broadband solution with global impact. 

 Creativity, problem-solving, and evaluating engineering trade-offs are all must-haves for this role. Audacious goals require persistent cleverness. 

 Our tools are critical to developer productivity, which means architecting a scalable, fault-tolerant system is essential. You will engage with experienced, cross-disciplinary staff to conceive and design innovative solutions. You must be detail oriented, responsive, flexible and able to succeed within an open collaborative environment. You will take part in designing solutions to hard problems, alongside a team of the world’s best engineers working toward one shared goal: seeing our satellites launching to form the world’s next global communications network.
