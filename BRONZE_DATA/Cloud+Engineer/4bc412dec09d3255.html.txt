We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Back-End AWS Cloud Engineer to join our client's dynamic engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining the back-end infrastructure and services that power our cloud-based applications. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure the scalability, reliability, and performance of our AWS-based systems. This position offers a hybrid onsite schedule of 3 flexible days at their office in Redmond, WA. 

 
Key Responsibilities:
 
 
 Design, develop, and deploy scalable and reliable back-end services and APIs on the AWS cloud platform. 
 Collaborate with front-end developers, DevOps engineers, and other stakeholders to define and implement RESTful APIs and microservices architectures. 
 Build and maintain cloud-native solutions that are highly available, fault-tolerant, and optimized for performance. 
 Monitor and troubleshoot production systems to ensure smooth operation, quick resolution of issues, and minimal downtime. 
 Implement security best practices to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and services. 
 Optimize and fine-tune application and database performance to meet stringent SLAs. 
 Automate deployment, scaling, and management of cloud resources using Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) tools. 
 Stay current with industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies related to AWS cloud services and back-end development. 
 Participate in code reviews, architectural discussions, and technical documentation. 
 
Qualifications:
 
 
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience). 
 Proven experience in designing, developing, and deploying back-end services on the AWS platform. 
 Strong proficiency in at least one modern back-end programming language (e.g., Python, Java, Node.js). 
 In-depth knowledge of AWS services such as EC2, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, S3, CloudWatch, and IAM. 
 Familiarity with serverless architecture, containerization (Docker), and orchestration tools (e.g., Kubernetes) 
 Experience with relational and NoSQL databases, data modeling, and optimization techniques preferred 
 Solid understanding of RESTful API design principles and microservices architectures. 
 Proficiency in version control systems (e.g., Git) and collaborative development workflows. 
 Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to diagnose and resolve complex technical issues. 
 Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, and the ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams. 
 Familiar with networking and Linux/Unix are preferred but not required 
 AWS certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Solutions Architect, AWS Certified Developer) are a plus. 
 
Additional Information:
 
 
 This is a full-time, hybrid onsite position based in Redmond, WA. 
 Very stable company that is growing rapidly 
 Opportunity to work in a dynamic, innovative, and collaborative environment. 
 Multiple bonus opportunities a year based on performance 
 5% match on 401k and strong benefits 
 Client cannot provide sponsorship & no C2C Engagements
